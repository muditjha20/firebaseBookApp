Welcome to Leafy Link

Here you can find various plant species to purchase from a marketplace!

How it works:

1. Click on the "Plant List" button to see a list of available plants.

2. Click the search bar to search for the specific plant you want.

3. Click "Add to Marketplace".

4. A prompt to enter your name and phone number should appear. Once you've entered, your plant will be added to the "Marketplace" section with your name and phone number attached.
